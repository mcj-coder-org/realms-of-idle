# Hurricane

## Overview

Summon a devastating hurricane of wind that strikes enemies from multiple directions, dealing damage and pushing them away.

| Property  | Value         |
| --------- | ------------- |
| School    | Transmutation |
| Element   | Air           |
| Targeting | Point (AoE)   |
| Classes   | Aeromancer    |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 80-100 air damage
- 14m radius
- 2s cast time
- Push distance: 8m
- Vulnerability: -10% air resistance for 6s
- 25 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 130-160 air damage
- 18m radius
- 1.5s cast time
- Push distance: 12m
- Vulnerability: -20% air resistance for 8s
- Airborne enemies take +30% damage from hurricane
- 25 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 180-220 air damage
- 22m radius
- 1s cast time
- Push distance: 16m
- Vulnerability: -35% air resistance for 10s
- Airborne enemies take +50% damage
- Creates sustained wind field (8s)
- 25 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
