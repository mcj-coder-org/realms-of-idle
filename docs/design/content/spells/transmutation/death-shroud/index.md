# Death Shroud

## Overview

Cloak yourself in a shroud of death that reduces incoming damage and damages enemies who dare approach.

| Property  | Value           |
| --------- | --------------- |
| School    | Transmutation   |
| Element   | Undead          |
| Targeting | Self            |
| Duration  | Until cancelled |
| Classes   | Necromancer     |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Damage reduction: 15%
- Damage to nearby enemies: 3-5 HP per second
- Effect radius: 6m
- Duration: Until cancelled
- Mana drain: 1 per second
- Instant cast
- 15 second cooldown (when started fresh)
- **Scaling**: +0.5% reduction per class level

### Greater

- Damage reduction: 25%
- Damage to nearby enemies: 6-8 HP per second
- Effect radius: 8m
- Makes nearby enemies weaker (reduced damage output by 10%)
- Duration: Until cancelled
- Mana drain: 0.75 per second
- Instant cast
- 15 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +0.75% reduction per class level

### Enhanced

- Damage reduction: 40%
- Damage to nearby enemies: 10-15 HP per second
- Effect radius: 10m
- Makes nearby enemies weaker (reduced damage output by 20%)
- Nearby enemies deal reduced healing effects
- Duration: Until cancelled
- Mana drain: 0.5 per second
- Instant cast
- 15 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +1% reduction per class level
