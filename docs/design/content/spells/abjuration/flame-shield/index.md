# Flame Shield

## Overview

Surround yourself with a shield of protective flame that absorbs damage and reflects fire attacks back at those who dare strike you.

| Property  | Value          |
| --------- | -------------- |
| School    | Abjuration     |
| Element   | Fire           |
| Targeting | Self or ally   |
| Duration  | Until depleted |
| Classes   | Pyromancer     |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Absorbs 50-70 damage
- Instant cast
- 45 second maximum duration
- Reflects 25% of fire damage to melee attackers
- 20 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 absorption per class level

### Greater

- Absorbs 90-120 damage
- Instant cast
- 60 second maximum duration
- Reflects 40% of fire damage to melee attackers
- Damages melee attackers: 5 HP per hit
- 20 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +6 absorption per class level

### Enhanced

- Absorbs 140-180 damage
- Instant cast
- 90 second maximum duration
- Reflects 60% of fire damage to melee attackers
- Damages melee attackers: 10 HP per hit
- Can redirect incoming fire spells
- 20 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +8 absorption per class level
