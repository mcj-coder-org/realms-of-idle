# Water Shield

## Overview

Surround yourself with a shield of healing water that absorbs damage and gradually restores your health.

| Property  | Value          |
| --------- | -------------- |
| School    | Abjuration     |
| Element   | Water          |
| Targeting | Self or ally   |
| Duration  | Until depleted |
| Classes   | Hydromancer    |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Absorbs 60-80 damage
- Healing per second: 3 HP
- Instant cast
- 40 second duration
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +3 absorption per class level

### Greater

- Absorbs 100-130 damage
- Healing per second: 6 HP
- Instant cast
- 60 second duration
- Reflects 15% water damage to attackers
- Can extend to nearby ally
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 absorption per class level

### Enhanced

- Absorbs 150-190 damage
- Healing per second: 10 HP
- Instant cast
- 90 second duration
- Reflects 30% water damage to attackers
- Heals nearby allies for 50% effect
- Increases resistance to water damage by 20%
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +5 absorption per class level
