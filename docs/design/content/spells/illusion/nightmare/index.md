# Nightmare

## Overview

Assault the minds of enemies with horrifying illusions that damage their psyche and reduce their combat effectiveness.

| Property  | Value       |
| --------- | ----------- |
| School    | Illusion    |
| Element   | Mind        |
| Targeting | Point (AoE) |
| Classes   | Illusionist |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 60-80 mental damage
- Affects 8m radius
- Fear effect: Enemies move randomly for 3s
- Reduced accuracy: -15%
- 2s cast time
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 110-140 mental damage
- Affects 12m radius
- Fear effect: Enemies move randomly for 4s
- Reduced accuracy: -30%
- Reduced damage output: -15%
- Panicked enemies can flee or become aggressive
- 1.5s cast time
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 170-210 mental damage
- Affects 16m radius
- Fear effect: Enemies move randomly for 6s, cannot act
- Reduced accuracy: -50%
- Reduced damage output: -30%
- Panicked enemies lose coordination with allies
- Can propagate fear to nearby panicked enemies
- 1s cast time
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
