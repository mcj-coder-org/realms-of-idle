# Tidal Wave

## Overview

Summon a massive wave of water that crashes down on enemies, dealing damage and leaving them disoriented.

| Property  | Value       |
| --------- | ----------- |
| School    | Destruction |
| Element   | Water       |
| Targeting | Point (AoE) |
| Classes   | Hydromancer |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 85-105 water damage
- 16m radius
- 2s cast time
- Stun duration: 2s
- Healing to caster: 20% of damage dealt
- 28 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 140-170 water damage
- 20m radius
- 1.5s cast time
- Stun duration: 3s
- Healing to caster: 35% of damage dealt
- Reduces enemy move speed by 30% after stun
- 28 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 200-240 water damage
- 24m radius
- 1s cast time
- Stun duration: 4s
- Healing to caster: 50% of damage dealt
- Reduces enemy move speed by 50% after stun
- Creates difficult terrain (slippery water)
- 28 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
