# Thunderstorm

## Overview

Summon a devastating storm of lightning that strikes all targets within the area, with chains arcing between nearby enemies. Thunderstorm represents the pinnacle of Electromancer area-effect magic.

| Property  | Value       |
| --------- | ----------- |
| School    | Destruction |
| Element   | Lightning   |
| Targeting | Point (AoE) |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 65-85 lightning damage
- 20m radius
- 3s cast time
- Chains to 3 nearby enemies (20-30 damage each)
- 15% stun chance (2s stun)
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 130-160 lightning damage
- 25m radius
- 2s cast time
- Chains to 5 nearby enemies (40-60 damage each)
- 30% stun chance (2-3s stun)
- Overload effect: Enemies affected take +10% lightning damage for 10s
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 180-220 lightning damage
- 30m radius
- 1.5s cast time
- Chains to 7 nearby enemies (60-90 damage each)
- 50% stun chance (3-4s stun)
- Overload effect: Enemies affected take +20% lightning damage for 15s
- Chain reaction: Each stunned enemy generates additional chains to nearby targets
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
