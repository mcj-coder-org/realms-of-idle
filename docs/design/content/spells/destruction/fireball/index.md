# Fireball

## Overview

Launch a ball of fire that explodes on impact, dealing fire damage to all targets in the area.

| Property  | Value       |
| --------- | ----------- |
| School    | Destruction |
| Element   | Fire        |
| Targeting | Point (AoE) |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 50-70 fire damage
- 3m radius
- 2s cast time
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 85-110 fire damage
- 5m radius
- 1.5s cast time
- Burning: 10 damage/s for 3s
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 120-150 fire damage
- 7m radius
- 1s cast time
- Burning: 15 damage/s for 5s
- Ignites flammable objects
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
