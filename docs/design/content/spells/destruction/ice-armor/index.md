# Ice Armor

## Overview

Encase yourself or a target in protective crystalline ice that absorbs damage and slows melee attackers who strike against it.

| Property  | Value                 |
| --------- | --------------------- |
| School    | Destruction           |
| Element   | Ice                   |
| Targeting | Self or single target |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Shield: Absorbs up to 80-100 damage
- Slow: Melee attackers -30% speed for 2 seconds on hit
- Duration: 60 seconds or until depleted
- Cast Time: 1 second
- Cooldown: 20 seconds
- **Scaling**: +2 absorption per class level

### Greater

- Shield: Absorbs up to 120-150 damage
- Slow: Melee attackers -50% speed for 3 seconds on hit
- Reflects: 15% of absorbed damage back to attacker
- Duration: 90 seconds or until depleted
- Cast Time: 1 second
- Cooldown: 18 seconds
- **Scaling**: +3 absorption per class level

### Enhanced

- Shield: Absorbs up to 160-200 damage
- Slow: Melee attackers -70% speed for 4 seconds on hit
- Reflects: 25% of absorbed damage back to attacker
- Freeze Chance: 25% to freeze attacker for 2s
- Duration: 120 seconds or until depleted
- Cast Time: 0.75 seconds
- Cooldown: 15 seconds
- **Scaling**: +4 absorption per class level
