# Earthquake

## Overview

Shake the earth violently, causing tremors and ruptures that damage all enemies in a large area and create difficult terrain.

| Property  | Value       |
| --------- | ----------- |
| School    | Destruction |
| Element   | Earth       |
| Targeting | Point (AoE) |
| Classes   | Geomancer   |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 75-95 earth damage
- 15m radius
- 2s cast time
- Creates difficult terrain for 8 seconds
- Knocked down chance: 30%
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +2 damage per class level

### Greater

- 125-155 earth damage
- 20m radius
- 1.5s cast time
- Creates difficult terrain for 12 seconds
- Knocked down chance: 50%
- Enemies lose balance and move slower
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +3 damage per class level

### Enhanced

- 180-220 earth damage
- 25m radius
- 1s cast time
- Creates difficult terrain for 20 seconds
- Knocked down chance: 75%
- Enemies lose balance, move slower, and take increased damage
- Can rupture the earth, creating barriers
- 30 second cooldown
- **Scaling**: +4 damage per class level
