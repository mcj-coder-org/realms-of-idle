# Cold Blessing

## Overview

Invoke ice spirits to bless allies with protective chill, granting freeze resistance and cooling relief.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Ice               |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Protection: +15% freeze resistance
- Cooling: +10% stamina regeneration
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 80 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +0.5% per Frost Shaman level

### Greater

- Protection: +30% freeze resistance
- Cooling: +20% stamina regeneration
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 150 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Additional: -20% damage taken from fire
- Scaling: +1% per Frost Shaman level

### Enhanced

- Protection: +50% freeze resistance
- Cooling: +35% stamina regeneration
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 250 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Additional: -40% damage taken from fire
- Clarity aura: Nearby allies gain +15% accuracy
- Scaling: +1.5% per Frost Shaman level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement              |
| ------ | ------------------------ |
| Spell  | Frost Shaman (automatic) |
