# Earth Blessing

## Overview

Invoke earth spirits to bless allies with protective fortification and stability.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Earth             |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Armor: +10 physical defense
- Stability: +15% knockback resistance
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 85 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +0.5 per Earth Shaman level

### Greater

- Armor: +20 physical defense
- Stability: +35% knockback resistance
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 155 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Additional: +25% damage to melee attacks
- Scaling: +1 per Earth Shaman level

### Enhanced

- Armor: +40 physical defense
- Stability: +60% knockback resistance
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 260 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Additional: +50% damage to melee attacks
- Earthbound: Allies cannot be knocked back or moved by spells
- Scaling: +1.5 per Earth Shaman level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement              |
| ------ | ------------------------ |
| Spell  | Earth Shaman (automatic) |
