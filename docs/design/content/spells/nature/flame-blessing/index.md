# Flame Blessing

## Overview

Invoke fire spirits to bless allies with spiritual warmth, protecting them from cold and granting temporary resilience.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Fire              |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Protection: +15% cold resistance
- Warmth: +10% health regeneration
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 80 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +0.5% per Flame Keeper level

### Greater

- Protection: +30% cold resistance
- Warmth: +20% health regeneration
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 150 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Additional: +5 base damage to fire spells
- Scaling: +1% per Flame Keeper level

### Enhanced

- Protection: +50% cold resistance
- Warmth: +35% health regeneration
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 250 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Additional: +10 base damage to fire spells
- Healing aura: Nearby allies gain +5% of your healing
- Scaling: +1.5% per Flame Keeper level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement              |
| ------ | ------------------------ |
| Spell  | Flame Keeper (automatic) |
