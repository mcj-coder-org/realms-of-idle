# Spirit Beast

## Overview

Call upon animal spirits to manifest as powerful beast allies that fight at your command.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Spiritual         |
| Targeting | Area / Summon     |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Summons: 1 animal spirit ally
- Beast type: Wolf, Bear, or Eagle
- Beast duration: 20 seconds
- Beast HP: 30% of caster HP
- Range: 20m
- Cost: 150 Spirit Pool
- Scaling: +5% per Beast Speaker level

### Greater

- Summons: 2 animal spirit allies
- Beast types: Different animals or same
- Beast duration: 30 seconds
- Beast HP: 60% of caster HP
- Range: 25m
- Cost: 250 Spirit Pool
- Pack bonuses: Allies coordinate attacks for +15% damage
- Scaling: +10% per Beast Speaker level

### Enhanced

- Summons: 3 animal spirit allies
- Beast types: Variety available (predators/guardians)
- Beast duration: 40 seconds
- Beast HP: 120% of caster HP
- Range: 30m
- Cost: 370 Spirit Pool
- Pack bonuses: Allies coordinate attacks for +30% damage
- Apex predator: One summon gains doubled stats
- Scaling: +15% per Beast Speaker level
