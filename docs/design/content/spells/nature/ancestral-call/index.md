# Ancestral Call

## Overview

Call upon ancestral spirits to manifest and aid your tribe in battle and times of need.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Spiritual         |
| Targeting | Area / Summon     |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Summons: 1 ancestral spirit ally
- Spirit duration: 20 seconds
- Spirit HP: 25% of caster HP
- Range: 20m
- Cost: 140 Spirit Pool
- Scaling: +5% per Ancestor Shaman level

### Greater

- Summons: 2 ancestral spirit allies
- Spirit duration: 30 seconds
- Spirit HP: 50% of caster HP
- Range: 25m
- Cost: 240 Spirit Pool
- Spirit power: Ancestors grant +10% damage to nearby allies
- Scaling: +10% per Ancestor Shaman level

### Enhanced

- Summons: 3 ancestral spirit allies
- Spirit duration: 40 seconds
- Spirit HP: 100% of caster HP
- Range: 30m
- Cost: 350 Spirit Pool
- Spirit power: Ancestors grant +20% damage to nearby allies
- Collective wisdom: All summons share buffs and effects
- Scaling: +15% per Ancestor Shaman level
