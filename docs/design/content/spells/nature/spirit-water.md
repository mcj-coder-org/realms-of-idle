# Spirit Water

## Overview

Call upon water spirits to manifest healing currents or flooding waters that damage enemies.

| Property  | Value                |
| --------- | -------------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic)    |
| Element   | Water                |
| Targeting | Area / Single Target |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Damage: 18-28 HP
- Healing alternative: 20-30 HP to allies
- Range: 20m
- Area: 8m radius
- Cost: 115 Spirit Pool
- Duration: Instant
- Scaling: +0.9 damage per Tide Caller level

### Greater

- Damage: 36-54 HP
- Healing alternative: 40-60 HP to allies
- Range: 25m
- Area: 12m radius
- Cost: 200 Spirit Pool
- Duration: Instant
- Purification: Removes minor poisons
- Scaling: +1.8 damage per Tide Caller level

### Enhanced

- Damage: 72-108 HP
- Healing alternative: 80-120 HP to allies
- Range: 30m
- Area: 15m radius
- Cost: 310 Spirit Pool
- Duration: Instant
- Purification: Removes poisons and curses
- Adaptation: Nearby allies gain water breathing
- Scaling: +3 damage per Tide Caller level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement             |
| ------ | ----------------------- |
| Spell  | Tide Caller (automatic) |
