# Water Blessing

## Overview

Invoke water spirits to bless allies with healing waters and aquatic adaptations.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Water             |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Healing: +15% health regeneration
- Cleansing: +25% poison resistance
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 85 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +0.7% per Tide Caller level

### Greater

- Healing: +30% health regeneration
- Cleansing: +50% poison resistance
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 155 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Additional: Can breathe underwater
- Scaling: +1.2% per Tide Caller level

### Enhanced

- Healing: +50% health regeneration
- Cleansing: +75% poison resistance
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 260 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Additional: Full aquatic adaptation and mobility
- Fluidity: Blessed can pass through narrow spaces
- Scaling: +1.8% per Tide Caller level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement             |
| ------ | ----------------------- |
| Spell  | Tide Caller (automatic) |
