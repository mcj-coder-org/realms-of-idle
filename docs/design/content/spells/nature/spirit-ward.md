# Spirit Ward

## Overview

Create protective barriers infused with ancestral power to defend sacred locations and allies.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Spiritual         |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Protection: +15% damage reduction
- Ward radius: 10m
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 100 Spirit Pool
- Scaling: +1% per Ancestor Shaman level

### Greater

- Protection: +30% damage reduction
- Ward radius: 15m
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 180 Spirit Pool
- Ancestral blessing: Healing spells gain +25% effectiveness
- Scaling: +1.5% per Ancestor Shaman level

### Enhanced

- Protection: +50% damage reduction
- Ward radius: 20m
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 290 Spirit Pool
- Ancestral blessing: Healing spells gain +50% effectiveness
- Intangibility: Ward blocks spiritual and magical attacks
- Scaling: +2% per Ancestor Shaman level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement                 |
| ------ | --------------------------- |
| Spell  | Ancestor Shaman (automatic) |
