# Air Blessing

## Overview

Invoke air spirits to bless allies with enhanced movement and freedom from restrictions.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Air               |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Speed: +20% movement speed
- Freedom: Can pass through minor obstacles
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 80 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +1% per Wind Walker level

### Greater

- Speed: +40% movement speed
- Freedom: Can pass through most obstacles
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 150 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Additional: +10% evasion
- Scaling: +1.5% per Wind Walker level

### Enhanced

- Speed: +60% movement speed
- Freedom: Ignore difficult terrain completely
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 250 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Additional: +20% evasion
- Untethered: Cannot be slowed or rooted
- Scaling: +2% per Wind Walker level

| Spell  | Wind Walker (automatic) |
