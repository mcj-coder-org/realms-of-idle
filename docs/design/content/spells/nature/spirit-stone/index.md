# Spirit Stone

## Overview

Call upon earth spirits to manifest stone constructs that block and damage enemies.

| Property  | Value                |
| --------- | -------------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic)    |
| Element   | Earth                |
| Targeting | Area / Single Target |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Damage: 20-30 HP
- Barrier: Creates 30 HP protective walls
- Range: 20m
- Area: 8m radius
- Cost: 125 Spirit Pool
- Duration: 5 seconds
- Scaling: +1 damage per Earth Shaman level

### Greater

- Damage: 40-60 HP
- Barrier: Creates 60 HP protective walls
- Range: 25m
- Area: 12m radius
- Cost: 210 Spirit Pool
- Duration: 8 seconds
- Knockdown: 30% chance to knock enemies down
- Scaling: +2 damage per Earth Shaman level

### Enhanced

- Damage: 80-120 HP
- Barrier: Creates 120 HP protective walls
- Range: 30m
- Area: 15m radius
- Cost: 330 Spirit Pool
- Duration: 10 seconds
- Knockdown: 60% chance to knock enemies down
- Entangle: Enemies in area move -50% speed
- Scaling: +3 damage per Earth Shaman level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement              |
| ------ | ------------------------ |
| Spell  | Earth Shaman (automatic) |
