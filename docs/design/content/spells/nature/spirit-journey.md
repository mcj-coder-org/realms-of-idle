# Spirit Journey

## Overview

Undertake a profound spiritual journey through multiple realms to gain power, knowledge, and connection.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Spiritual         |
| Targeting | Self / Group      |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Journey type: Guided spiritual travel
- Duration: Ritual lasts 15 seconds
- Cost: 160 Spirit Pool
- Benefit: Gain +20% to all stats for 10 minutes
- Allies: Can take 1 willing ally
- Scaling: +5% per Spirit Walker level

### Greater

- Journey type: Multi-realm spiritual travel
- Duration: Ritual lasts 25 seconds
- Cost: 270 Spirit Pool
- Benefit: Gain +40% to all stats for 15 minutes
- Allies: Can take up to 2 willing allies
- Insight: All group members gain +30% XP for 15 minutes
- Scaling: +10% per Spirit Walker level

### Enhanced

- Journey type: Full transcendent spiritual travel
- Duration: Ritual lasts 35 seconds
- Cost: 400 Spirit Pool
- Benefit: Gain +60% to all stats for 20 minutes
- Allies: Can take up to 4 willing allies
- Insight: All group members gain +50% XP for 20 minutes
- Reconnection: Restore all resources and remove all debuffs
- Scaling: +15% per Spirit Walker level
