# Barkskin

## Overview

Transform skin into tough bark-like material, providing natural armor.

| Property  | Value         |
| --------- | ------------- |
| School    | Nature        |
| Element   | Earth         |
| Targeting | Self or Touch |

## Effects

### Lesser

- +15 armor
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Slight movement penalty (-5%)
- **Scaling**: +1 armor per level

### Greater

- +25 armor
- Duration: 10 minutes
- No movement penalty
- +10% resistance to slashing
- **Scaling**: +2 armor per level

### Innate (Creature)

- Permanent effect for nature creatures
- Part of creature's natural state
- Armor value varies by creature

## Notes

- Vulnerable to fire (+25% fire damage while active)
- Cannot be dispelled by normal means
- Visual effect: skin takes on wood-grain texture
- Can be cast on willing allies
