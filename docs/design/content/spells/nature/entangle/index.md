# Entangle

## Overview

Cause plants to grow rapidly and ensnare targets, restricting movement.

| Property  | Value         |
| --------- | ------------- |
| School    | Nature        |
| Element   | Earth         |
| Targeting | Area (ground) |

## Effects

### Lesser

- 5m radius
- Slows movement by 50%
- Duration: 10s
- **Scaling**: +1s per level

### Greater

- 8m radius
- Roots target in place (immobile)
- Duration: 8s
- Strength check to break free
- **Scaling**: +1s per level, +5% hold strength

### Innate (Creature)

- Used by nature creatures
- Range and power varies by creature
- No mana cost for creature

## Notes

- Requires natural ground (dirt, grass, forest floor)
- Does not work on stone or metal surfaces
- Fire damage destroys entangling plants
- Multiple casts do not stack
