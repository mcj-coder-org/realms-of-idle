# Animal Blessing

## Overview

Invoke animal spirits to bless allies with primal instincts, speed, and hunting prowess.

| Property  | Value             |
| --------- | ----------------- |
| School    | Nature (Shamanic) |
| Element   | Spiritual         |
| Targeting | Area / Self       |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Instinct: +20% perception and senses
- Speed: +15% attack speed
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Cost: 90 Spirit Pool
- Range: 20m
- Scaling: +1% per Beast Speaker level

### Greater

- Instinct: +40% perception and senses
- Speed: +30% attack speed
- Duration: 20 minutes
- Cost: 165 Spirit Pool
- Range: 25m
- Predatory: +20% damage to wounded enemies
- Scaling: +1.5% per Beast Speaker level

### Enhanced

- Instinct: +60% perception and senses
- Speed: +50% attack speed
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Cost: 280 Spirit Pool
- Range: 30m
- Predatory: +40% damage to wounded enemies
- Feral: Cannot be slowed or feared, gain pack bonus +25% if near allies
- Scaling: +2% per Beast Speaker level

## Acquisition

| Method | Requirement               |
| ------ | ------------------------- |
| Spell  | Beast Speaker (automatic) |
