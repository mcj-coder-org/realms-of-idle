# Combat Enchantments

Offensive enchantments that add elemental damage, divine power, or life-stealing capabilities to weapons and combat gear. Many also have practical utility applications for non-combat users.

## Available Enchantments

| Enchantment               | Tier       | Summary                                                 | Cost Range  |
| ------------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| [Flaming](flaming.md)     | Journeyman | Fire damage + utility (cooking, light, warmth)          | 150-20000c  |
| [Frost](frost.md)         | Journeyman | Cold damage + slowing + utility (preservation, cooling) | 150-20000c  |
| [Lightning](lightning.md) | Journeyman | Electric damage + chain effect + utility (signaling)    | 150-20000c  |
| [Holy](holy.md)           | Master     | Divine radiant damage vs evil/undead (restricted)       | 500-100000c |
| [Vampiric](vampiric.md)   | Master     | Life drain healing (licensed, regulated)                | 400-80000c  |

## Category Overview

**Primary Users:** Adventurers, guards, soldiers (combat); also craftspeople, travelers (utility)

**Unique Feature:** Combat enchantments have dual purpose - offensive capability AND everyday utility applications.

**Utility Applications:**

- **Flaming:** Fire starting, cooking, emergency light, welding, thawing frozen items
- **Frost:** Food preservation, summer cooling for bakers, ice creation
- **Lightning:** Emergency signaling, quick metal heating, electrical work
- **Holy:** Light source, blessing ceremonies, evil detection
- **Vampiric:** Emergency healing (combat situations only)

**Best Combinations:**

- Sharpness + Any Combat = Enhanced damage weapon
- Vampiric + Regeneration = Extreme healing build
- Lightning + Frost = Area control (slow + damage)
- Holy + Regeneration = Paladin/cleric standard

**Element Conflicts:**

- Cannot combine Flaming + Frost on same item (opposition)
- Choose one primary element per weapon
- Can have different elements on different equipped items

**Budget Recommendations:**

- Combat starter: Flaming II (800c) or Frost II (800c)
- Professional soldier: Level III + Sharpness (4000c+)
- Utility user: Level I (150c) for practical applications
- Master combatant: Vampiric III or Holy III (15000c+)

**Legal Notes:**

- **Holy:** Requires alignment/virtue requirements, religious institution approval
- **Vampiric:** Licensed use only, registration required, periodic renewal
- **All Combat:** May require permits in peaceful regions
