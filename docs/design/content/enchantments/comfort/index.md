# Comfort Enchantments

Enchantments focused on making daily life more comfortable - perfect fit clothing, climate control, and self-cleaning items. Universal appeal across all professions and social classes. These are the "cozy" enchantments that define quality of life.

## Available Enchantments

| Enchantment                   | Tier       | Summary                                      | Cost Range |

| [Comfort](comfort.md)         | Apprentice | Perfect fit, pressure relief, breathability  | 20-8000c   |

## Category Overview

**Primary Users:** Everyone (truly universal appeal)

**ROI Leader:** Cleanliness - pays for itself in 6 months through water, soap, and time savings.

**The Perfect Wardrobe Combo:**

- **Durability + Cleanliness + Comfort** = Clothes that last forever, never get dirty, always feel perfect
- **Cost:** ~315c for Level II of each (excellent value)
- **Result:** Buy once, wear for life

**Individual Benefits:**

**Comfort:**

- No blisters, chafing, or pressure points
- Perfect fit regardless of body shape
- Stamina regeneration bonus (+5% to +30%)
- Mental health benefit (reduced daily friction)
- Universal application (all clothing 100% effective)

**Cleanliness:**

- Self-cleaning (Level I: basic, Level V: antiseptic)
- Stain prevention and removal
- Odor elimination
- Disease prevention (Level III+)
- Water savings: 70-80% reduction
- Environmental benefit: massive reduction in textile waste
- Public health standard: Level II food service, Level V medical

**Best Combinations:**

- **Perfect Wardrobe:** Durability + Cleanliness + Comfort (eternal comfort)
- **Climate Master:** Warmth/Cooling + Comfort + Cleanliness (perfect environment)
- **Professional Appearance:** Cleanliness + Comfort + Presence (always look your best)
- **Travel Essential:** All Comfort enchantments + Swift Step (comfortable journey)

**Budget Recommendations:**

- **Tight Budget:** Cleanliness I (15c) - biggest immediate impact
- **Working Class:** Cleanliness II (60c) + Comfort I (20c) = 80c quality of life
- **Middle Class:** Full Perfect Wardrobe Level II (315c) - lifetime value
- **Wealthy:** All at Level III-IV for ultimate daily comfort (1000c+)

**Economic Impact:**

- **Household Savings:** Cleanliness eliminates most laundry costs
- **Time Savings:** No laundry days (reclaim 4-8 hours weekly)
- **Water Infrastructure:** Cities with widespread Cleanliness use 20-30% less water
- **Textile Industry:** Shifted from quantity to quality focus
- **Public Health:** Cleanliness III+ in schools reduced illness transmission significantly

**Social Impacts:**

- **Accessibility:** Elderly/disabled benefit from reduced laundry burden
- **Economic Equity:** Professional appearance no longer indicates wealth
- **Environmental:** Massive reduction in water use and chemical discharge
- **Quality of Life:** Universal improvement in daily comfort and hygiene

**Special Considerations:**

- **Warmth/Cooling:** Cannot combine on same item (temperature conflict)
- **Cleanliness:** Personal hygiene still required (enchantment doesn't replace bathing)
- **Comfort:** Adaptation period minimal (feels great immediately)
- **Climate:** Choose Warmth or Cooling based on primary regional climate
- **Recharge:** Every 2-5 years (Apprentice tier, frequent use)

**Profession Applications:**

- **Universal:** Everyone benefits from Comfort suite
- **Outdoor Workers:** Warmth essential (northern regions)
- **Hot Profession:** Cooling essential (blacksmiths, bakers, glass blowers)
- **Medical/Food Service:** Cleanliness III+ required by regulation
- **Children:** Cleanliness II+ parent sanity saver
- **Travelers:** All four for ultimate journey comfort
