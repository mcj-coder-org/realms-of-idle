# Longsword

## Overview

Forge a longsword with three-foot blade, extended grip, and full crossguard. The archetypal weapon of warriors.

## Process

1. **Heat Metal** - Bring all three ingots to temperature
2. **Weld Billet** - Forge weld ingots together for blade length
3. **Draw Out Blade** - Hammer to 3-foot length
4. **Taper Blade** - Shape distal taper toward point
5. **Form Fuller** - Create groove for weight reduction
6. **Forge Crossguard** - Shape and attach guard
7. **Form Tang** - Create extension for pommel
8. **Sharpen Edge** - Grind cutting edge
9. **Attach Grip** - Fit wood handle to tang
10. **Wrap Grip** - Wrap leather strips around handle
11. **Attach Pommel** - Peon tang to secure grip
12. **Quench** - Heat treat entire blade
13. **Finish** - Final sharpening, polish, and balance
