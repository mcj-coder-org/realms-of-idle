# Weapons

Weapons are arms for combat, hunting, and defense. They are crafted by smiths using refined materials and components.

## Weapon Types

- **[Dagger](dagger/)** - Short blade for close combat and utility
- **[Longsword](longsword/)** - Versatile sword for combat

## Crafting

| Weapon    | Recipe                                                | Materials                                  |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Dagger    | [Dagger](../../../recipe/blacksmithing/dagger/)       | Ingot x2, Leather Strip x1, Wood Handle x1 |
| Longsword | [Longsword](../../../recipe/blacksmithing/longsword/) | Ingot x3, Leather Strip x2, Wood Handle x1 |

## Material Variants

| Base Metal | Variant                 | Tier | Stats       |
| ---------- | ----------------------- | ---- | ----------- |
| Copper     | Copper Dagger/Longsword | 1    | -20% damage |
| Iron       | Iron Dagger/Longsword   | 2    | Standard    |
| Steel      | Steel Dagger/Longsword  | 3    | +25% damage |

## Related Classes

- **[Blacksmith](../../../classes/crafter/blacksmith/)** - Crafts weapons
- **[Fighter](../../../classes/fighter/)** - Uses weapons in combat
