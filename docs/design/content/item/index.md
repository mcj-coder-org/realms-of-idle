# Items

Items are the physical objects that characters interact with, craft, trade, and use. From raw materials to finished weapons, items are the tangible expression of the game's crafting and economic systems.

## Categories

- **[Raw](raw/)** - Unprocessed materials from gathering
- **[Processed](processed/)** - Refined materials ready for crafting
- **[Component](component/)** - Crafting parts and pieces
- **[Tool](tool/)** - Equipment for gathering and crafting
- **[Weapon](weapon/)** - Arms for combat and hunting
- **[Consumable](consumable/)** - Food, potions, and expendables

## Item Flow

```
[Gathering Class] → Raw Item → [Processing Recipe] → Component/Processed Item
                                                 ↓
                              [Crafting Class] + [Recipe] → Finished Item
```

## Related Classes

- **[Gatherers](../../classes/gatherer/)** - Acquire raw items
- **[Crafters](../../classes/crafter/)** - Create finished items
- **[Traders](../../classes/trader/)** - Buy and sell items
