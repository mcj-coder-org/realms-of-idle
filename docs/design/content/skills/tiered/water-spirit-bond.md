# Water Spirit Bond

## Overview

Forge a deep bond with the spirits of water and flowing currents, channeling their power through your shamanic connection.

| Property | Value       |
| -------- | ----------- |
| Pool     | Shamanic    |
| Category | Tiered      |
| Classes  | Tide Caller |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Spirit Power: +15% to water-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +20% spirit influence
- Duration: +30% for water blessings
- Scaling: +1% per Tide Caller level

### Greater

- Spirit Power: +30% to water-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +40% spirit influence
- Duration: +60% for water blessings
- Scaling: +1.5% per Tide Caller level

### Enhanced

- Spirit Power: +50% to water-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +70% spirit influence
- Duration: +120% for water blessings
- Scaling: +2% per Tide Caller level

## Acquisition

| Method      | Requirement                |
| ----------- | -------------------------- |
| Class Grant | Tide Caller (Lesser)       |
| Level Up    | Tide Caller 10+ (Greater)  |
| Level Up    | Tide Caller 25+ (Enhanced) |
