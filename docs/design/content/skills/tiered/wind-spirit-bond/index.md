# Wind Spirit Bond

## Overview

Forge a deep bond with the spirits of wind and air, channeling their freedom through your shamanic connection.

| Property | Value       |
| -------- | ----------- |
| Pool     | Shamanic    |
| Category | Tiered      |
| Classes  | Wind Walker |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Spirit Power: +15% to wind-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +20% spirit influence
- Duration: +30% for air blessings
- Scaling: +1% per Wind Walker level

### Greater

- Spirit Power: +30% to wind-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +40% spirit influence
- Duration: +60% for air blessings
- Scaling: +1.5% per Wind Walker level

### Enhanced

- Spirit Power: +50% to wind-based shamanic effects
- Connection Strength: +70% spirit influence
- Duration: +120% for air blessings
- Scaling: +2% per Wind Walker level

## Acquisition

| Method      | Requirement                |
| ----------- | -------------------------- |
| Class Grant | Wind Walker (Lesser)       |
| Level Up    | Wind Walker 10+ (Greater)  |
| Level Up    | Wind Walker 25+ (Enhanced) |
