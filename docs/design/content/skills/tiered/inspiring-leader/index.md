# Inspiring Leader

**Pool**: Leadership
**Type**: Tiered
**Classes**: Leader, Chieftain, Knight

## Description

Your leadership enhances the capabilities of those who follow you, improving their stats and morale through your presence and example.

## Tiers

| Tier     | Effect                                            | Range    | Scaling               |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------- | -------- | --------------------- |
| Lesser   | Followers +10% all stats                          | Medium   | +0.5% per class level |
| Greater  | Followers +18% stats, +15% morale                 | Far      | +0.4% per class level |
| Enhanced | Followers +25% stats, +25% morale, immune to fear | Very Far | +0.3% per class level |

## Acquisition

**Trigger Actions**:

- Lead followers
- Inspire through action

**Classes with Strong Synergy**: Leader, Chieftain, Knight (2x XP, direct specialization)

## XP-Based Progression

Skills improve continuously with accumulated XP. More XP = better effectiveness, lower costs, higher success rates.
