# Heat Reading

**Type:** Tiered
**Pool:** Crafting (Smithing)
**Strong Synergy Classes:** Blacksmith, Weaponsmith, Armorsmith, Miner

## Effect

| Tier     | Effect                                                        | Scaling                  |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Lesser   | Judge metal temperature by color; never overheat              | +0.3% accuracy per level |
| Greater  | Predict heat retention; work faster while maintaining quality | +0.2% per level          |
| Enhanced | Sense metal readiness intuitively; perfect timing always      | +0.1% per level          |

## Scaling Examples

**Blacksmith + Heat Reading (Strong Synergy)**

- Base effectiveness: Full
- Level 1-10: 100% accuracy + bonuses
- Level 25: +7.5% additional accuracy
- Level 50: +15% additional accuracy

## Trigger Actions

- Work with high-heat metals
- Maintain perfect heat cycles
- Achieve flawless forge work

## Notes

- Essential for preventing metal damage from overheating
- Allows working with delicate metals safely
- Lesser tier is foundational skill for smithing specialties
- Can also apply to mining/smelting operations
