# Tide Sense

## Overview

Attune yourself to water and fluid flows, gaining enhanced awareness and perception abilities.

| Property | Value       |
| -------- | ----------- |
| Pool     | Shamanic    |
| Category | Tiered      |
| Classes  | Tide Caller |

## Effects

### Lesser

- Perception: +20% sight range in wet environments
- Healing: +15% from water sources
- Duration: 10 seconds per activation
- Scaling: +1% per Tide Caller level

### Greater

- Perception: +40% sight range in wet environments
- Healing: +30% from water sources
- Duration: 20 seconds per activation
- Water sense: Can sense water sources up to 100m away
- Scaling: +1.5% per Tide Caller level

### Enhanced

- Perception: +60% sight range in wet environments
- Healing: +50% from water sources
- Duration: 30 seconds per activation
- Water sense: Can sense water sources up to 300m away
- Aquatic adaptation: Breathe underwater and move freely in water
- Scaling: +2% per Tide Caller level

## Acquisition

| Method      | Requirement                |
| ----------- | -------------------------- |
| Class Grant | Tide Caller (Lesser)       |
| Level Up    | Tide Caller 10+ (Greater)  |
| Level Up    | Tide Caller 25+ (Enhanced) |
