# Iron Stomach

**Pool**: Common
**Type**: Passive
**Universal Access**: Available to all classes

## Description

Stomach of steel allows you to eat questionable food without ill effects.

## Tiers

| Tier     | Base Effect                              | Scaling          |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Lesser   | +15% food poison resistance              | +1% per level    |
| Greater  | Eat spoiled food safely, +30% resistance | +0.75% per level |
| Enhanced | Immune to food poisoning, eat anything   | +0.5% per level  |

## Acquisition

**Trigger Actions**:

- Eat poor quality food
- Eat questionable items
- Survive food poisoning

**Influence Rate**: 0.5 per poor meal, 2.0 per food poisoning survived

## Scaling

Scales with total combined class levels (rewards generalists).
