# Thick Skin

**Pool**: Common
**Type**: Passive
**Universal Access**: Available to all classes

## Description

Naturally tough hide or hardened skin reduces incoming physical damage by a flat amount.

## Tiers

| Tier     | Base Effect                         | Scaling         |
| -------- | ----------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Lesser   | -2 flat damage from physical        | +0.2 per level  |
| Greater  | -4 flat damage from physical        | +0.15 per level |
| Enhanced | -6 flat damage, -5% critical damage | +0.1 per level  |

## Acquisition

**Trigger Actions**:

- Take physical damage
- Fight without heavy armor
- Endure hits

**Influence Rate**: 0.02 per hit taken, 0.2 per combat as damage sponge

## Scaling

Scales with total combined class levels (rewards generalists).
