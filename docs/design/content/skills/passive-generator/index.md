# Passive Generator Skills

**Type**: Passive Generator
**Description**: Skills that generate resources, opportunities, or benefits passively over time.

## Overview

Passive generator skills provide ongoing benefits without active player input. These skills generate valuable resources, unlock opportunities, or provide regular bonuses on a scheduled basis (daily, weekly, or monthly).

## Characteristics

- **Automatic Generation**: Benefits occur without player action
- **Time-Based**: Generate on a schedule (daily/weekly/monthly)
- **Resource Production**: Create tangible benefits (items, knowledge, contacts, etc.)
- **Scaling**: Many scale with skill tier or class level

## Acquisition

Passive generators are typically learned through:

- **Mastery**: Achieving high proficiency in a related skill or activity
- **Reputation**: Building strong connections in a trade or community
- **Deep Knowledge**: Extensive study or experience in a field
- **Natural Progression**: Advancing in a synergistic class

## Usage Notes

- Passive generators work best for dedicated specialists
- Benefits accumulate even when offline or inactive
- Synergy classes often receive enhanced generation rates
- Some generators may have storage limits or require collection
