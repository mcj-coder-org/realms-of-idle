# Ancient Knowledge

**Pool**: Scholar
**Type**: Passive Generator
**Classes**: Scholar

## Description

Your deep study of ancient texts occasionally surfaces forgotten knowledge, facts, and even lost techniques.

## Tiers

| Tier     | Monthly Effect                                  |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Lesser   | Recall 1-2 relevant obscure facts               |
| Greater  | Recall 2-4 facts, including useful applications |
| Enhanced | Recall 3-6 facts, may include lost techniques   |

## Acquisition

**Trigger Actions**:

- Study ancient texts
- Research history

**Classes with Strong Synergy**: Scholar (2x XP, direct specialization)

## Fixed Generation

Generation rate is fixed per tier - not affected by scaling. Quality and relevance improve with continued study.
