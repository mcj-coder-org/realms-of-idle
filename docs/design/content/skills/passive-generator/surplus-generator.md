# Surplus Generator

## Overview

**Type**: Passive Generator
**Pool**: Agriculture
**Description**: Farm produces extra yield over time with increasing quality bonuses.

## Synergy Classes

Classes with strong synergies: Farmer, Rancher

**Synergy Benefits**: 2x XP acquisition, enhanced effectiveness, reduced costs (scales with class level)

## Tier Progression

| Tier     | Weekly Effect                                                    |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Lesser   | Farm produces 5% extra yield passively                           |
| Greater  | 10% extra yield, occasional quality bonus                        |
| Enhanced | 15% extra yield, regular quality bonus, rare exceptional produce |

## XP Sources

- Maintain productive farm: 5-10 XP per week
- Optimize operations: 10-20 XP
- Guided training: 10-100 XP/session
- Unguided training: 5-50 XP/session

## Level-Up Availability

- Strong synergy: ~500 XP (~50 actions)
- Moderate synergy: ~1000 XP
- No synergy: ~1500 XP

## Synergy Examples

### Strong (2x XP)

**Farmer Level 15** - Production specialist

- 20% extra yield
- Enhanced quality
- 2x XP from farm optimization

### Moderate (1.5x XP)

**Rancher Level 10** - Farm manager

- 12% extra yield

### None (1x XP)

**Assassin** - No connection

- No bonuses
