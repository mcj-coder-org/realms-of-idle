# Light Fingers

**Type**: Passive
**Tags**: `Stealth`, `Dexterity`
**Typical Classes**: Rogue

## Description

Your hands are naturally quick and precise. Your fingers work with practiced grace to relieve others of their possessions without drawing notice. This passive skill grants you an inherent edge in sleight of hand operations.

## Mechanics

**Pickpocketing Bonus:**

- +5% success rate for pickpocketing
- Applies to all theft and sleight of hand attempts
- Stacks with other dexterity bonuses
- Always active, cannot be disabled

**Skill Benefits:**

- Faster hand movements
- Better finger coordination
- Reduced detection risk
- Natural dexterity enhancement

**Detection Avoidance:**

- Small theft attempts less likely to be noticed
- Lower risk of triggering alarms
- Quieter interactions with items
- More natural-looking movements

## Acquisition

**Class-based:**

- Auto-granted to Rogue class upon acceptance
- Foundational passive for theft and stealth playstyle

## Scaling

**No Scaling**: Fixed +5% bonus that does not improve with level.

## Interactions

**Works well with:**

- [Shadow Sense](../mechanic-unlock/shadow-sense/index.md) - Identify theft opportunities
- Stealth movement (avoid detection)
- Crowded areas (use cover effectively)
- High-value targets (maximize gain)

**Examples:**

- Rogue in marketplace: +5% to steal from merchants
- Rogue at noble event: +5% to extract valuables
- Rogue in battle: +5% to disarm or steal weapons

## Trade-offs

**Benefits:**

- Always-active bonus to stealth theft
- No skill requirement or training
- Works in any situation
- Stacks with enchantments

**Limitations:**

- Fixed bonus (doesn't scale)
- Only applies to theft/pickpocketing
- Doesn't prevent all detection
- May trigger moral consequences
