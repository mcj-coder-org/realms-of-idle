# Perfect Balance

**Type:** Mechanic Unlock
**Pool:** Smithing
**Strong Synergy Classes:** Weaponsmith, Warrior

## Effect

| Aspect    | Description                                               |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Unlock    | Craft weapons with perfectly balanced weight distribution |
| Technique | Center of gravity optimization in weapon design           |
| Quality   | Weapons deal +10% damage due to efficiency                |

## Mechanics

- Creates weapons with optimal handling
- Reduces weapon fatigue penalties
- Increases attack speed for heavy weapons
- Enables one-handed use of larger weapons

## Trigger Actions

- Craft weapons with weight considerations
- Create specialized weapon types
- Achieve perfect damage output
