# Crisis Leadership

**Pool**: Leadership
**Type**: Mechanic Unlock
**Classes**: Leader, Chieftain, Knight, Settlement Leader

## Description

Rise to the challenge when times are most dire, automatically recognizing crisis situations and doubling your leadership effectiveness.

## Effect

| Property  | Description                                        |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Unlock    | During crises, all leadership bonuses doubled      |
| Detection | Automatically recognize crisis situations          |
| Cooldown  | Cannot be constantly "in crisis" - must be genuine |

## Acquisition

**Trigger Actions**:

- Lead through crises
- Maintain composure under pressure

**Classes with Strong Synergy**: Leader, Chieftain, Knight, Settlement Leader (2x XP, direct specialization)

## No Scaling

Binary unlock - crisis detection and bonus doubling are fixed effects.
