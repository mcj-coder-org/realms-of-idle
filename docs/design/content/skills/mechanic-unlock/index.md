# Mechanic Unlock Skills

**Type**: Mechanic Unlock
**Description**: Skills that unlock new mechanics or capabilities, typically binary (you have it or you don't).

## Overview

Mechanic unlock skills provide entirely new capabilities rather than incremental improvements. Once learned, they grant access to actions, crafting options, or systems that were previously unavailable.

## Characteristics

- **Binary Acquisition**: Either you have the skill or you don't
- **No Tiers**: Most mechanic unlocks are single-level (some have multi-tier variants)
- **New Capabilities**: Opens up new gameplay mechanics
- **Prerequisite for Other Content**: May be required to access recipes, systems, or advanced skills

## Acquisition

Mechanic unlock skills are typically learned through:

- **Discovery**: Performing related actions that hint at the mechanic
- **Training**: Learning from an NPC who has the skill
- **Experimentation**: Attempting the locked mechanic and eventually succeeding
- **Quest Rewards**: Completing specific tasks or storylines

## Multi-Tier Unlocks

Some mechanic unlocks have multiple tiers that progressively add new capabilities:

- **Extortion** (2 tiers)
- **False Identity** (3 tiers)
- **Money Laundering** (2 tiers)
- **Delegation** (2 tiers)
- **Spirit Walk** (3 tiers)

These function as a series of related mechanic unlocks rather than scaling power.
