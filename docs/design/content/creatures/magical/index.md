# Magical Creatures

Creatures with innate magical abilities or elemental affinities.

## Categories

| Category       | Examples               | Abilities        |
| -------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------- |
| Elemental      | Fire imp, frost sprite | Elemental damage |
| Undead         | Skeleton, zombie       | See Death system |
| Magical beasts | Griffin, basilisk      | Special attacks  |
| Spirits        | Wisp, shade            | Incorporeal      |

## Special Harvestables

Magical creatures provide unique reagents:

- Elemental essence
- Magical cores
- Enchanted parts
- Ritual components

## Combat

Higher difficulty than wildlife. Often require specific strategies or resistances.
