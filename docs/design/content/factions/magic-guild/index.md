# Magic Guild

## Overview

The Magic Guild is the premier organization for magical practitioners across civilized lands. Founded centuries ago by the greatest mages of the age, it serves as both academy, governing body, and professional association for all who practice the arcane arts.

## Organization Structure

### Ranks and Progression

| Rank       | Requirements                      | Privileges                          |
| ---------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | Channeler class, Guild membership | Access to basic libraries, training |
| Journeyman | Mage class, 5,000 XP              | Access to restricted libraries      |
| Master     | Tier 3 specialization, 50,000 XP  | Can take apprentices, voting rights |
| Archmage   | Recognized expertise, nomination  | Council seat, policy making         |

### Specialization Councils

The Guild maintains specialized councils for major magical disciplines:

| Council     | Focus                     | Responsibilities                  |
| ----------- | ------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Elemental   | Fire, Ice, Lightning, etc | Fire safety, environmental impact |
| Conjuration | Summoning, undeath        | Creature binding, ethics          |
| Illusion    | Deception, perception     | Truth verification                |

## Services Provided

### Training and Education

- Structured curriculum for aspiring mages
- Advanced specialty training for Masters
- Continuing education seminars

### Library and Archives

- Spell research databases
- Historical magical texts
- Classified magical theory

### Mediation and Enforcement

- Resolves disputes between mages
- Enforces ethical guidelines
- Manages violations and sanctions

## Membership Benefits

| Benefit                      | Rank Required |
| ---------------------------- | ------------- |
| Guild discount on spells     | Apprentice    |
| Access to restricted library | Journeyman    |
| Can mentor apprentices       | Master        |
| Voting on Guild policy       | Master        |
| Revenue sharing on spells    | Journeyman    |
