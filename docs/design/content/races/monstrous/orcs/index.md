# Orcs

> **See [Base Traits](base.md)** for shared racial characteristics, lore, and cultural notes.

## Variants

Orcs have adapted to various environments across the world, each variant developing distinct tactical specializations and environmental adaptations while maintaining their core identity as honor-bound warriors with rigid codes of conduct.

### Plains Orc

Mounted warriors of the grasslands, representing the classic raider image. Masters of cavalry warfare who breed massive wolves and boars as war mounts, creating terrifying mobile forces that strike settlements and vanish before organized response.

**Key Features:** Mounted warfare mastery, +15% riding skill, cavalry tactics

**[Learn More →](plains.md)**

### Mountain Orc

Highland fortress defenders who occupy high strongholds and caves, defending territory rather than raiding. The most settled orc variant, maintaining permanent structures and occasionally trading (carefully) with nearby dwarven holds. They're the most defensive-minded orcs.

**Key Features:** Fortress defense, +20% cold resistance, defensive tactics

**[Learn More →](mountain.md)**

### Desert Orc

Lean, tough raiders adapted to harsh arid lands. Expert survivors who strike from desert wastes, take what they need, and vanish into territories others can't pursue. They're the most mobile and opportunistic orc variant.

**Key Features:** Desert raiding, +20% heat resistance, survival expertise

**[Learn More →](desert.md)**

### Swamp Orc

Guerrilla warfare specialists who developed cunning tactics in wetland environments. Smaller than other orcs but more clever, using terrain advantages to ambush larger forces. They're the most tactical and unconventional orc variant.

**Key Features:** Guerrilla tactics, +15% stealth in swamps, ambush mastery

**[Learn More →](swamp.md)**

## Choosing a Variant

### Choose Plains Orc if

- You want to play the quintessential mounted warrior raiding from the grasslands
- You value mobility, cavalry warfare, and traditional orc raiding tactics
- You enjoy charging into battle on massive war mounts (wolves, boars)
- You're drawn to classic orc imagery and traditional warrior culture
- You prefer aggressive, straightforward combat approaches
- Mounted combat and cavalry tactics appeal to your playstyle
- You want to lead war parties on swift raids

### Choose Mountain Orc if

- You prefer defensive tactics and fortress warfare over raiding
- Cold environments and high mountain strongholds appeal to you
- You want to play a more settled, pragmatic orc variant
- You value permanence and territory defense over mobility
- You're interested in the most "civilized" orc culture
- You enjoy playing defenders and hold guards
- You want potential for cross-cultural interactions with dwarves
- Environmental resistance to cold matters for your campaign

### Choose Desert Orc if

- Your adventures center on arid, desert, or wasteland environments
- You value survival skills, endurance, and opportunistic raiding
- You want to play a lean, tough, mobile raider
- Hit-and-run tactics and quick strikes appeal to you
- You're drawn to the harshest environments and toughest survivors
- Heat resistance is valuable for your campaign setting
- You prefer independence and self-reliance over tribal structure

### Choose Swamp Orc if

- You prefer guerrilla warfare and unconventional tactics
- Stealth and ambush strategies appeal more than direct combat
- Wetland environments are central to your campaign
- You want to play a smaller but more cunning orc
- You enjoy using terrain advantages and tactical thinking
- You're interested in subverting the "brutish orc" stereotype
- Swamp environments and stealth bonuses matter for your playstyle
- You value adaptability and clever solutions over raw strength

## Available Classes

Orcs have **unrestricted access to all classes** with strong affinity to warrior and shaman paths:

**All Classes Available**: Every class is accessible to orcs regardless of variant

- Orcs excel at: Warrior, Guard, Shaman (tribal/martial focused)
- Orcs can access: All crafting, gathering, magic, trading, and rogue classes

**Tribal Class Traditions**:

- **Shamanic Classes**: Deep spiritual heritage makes shamans and spirit magic natural paths
- **Warrior Classes**: Combat culture provides martial advantages and heritage bonuses
- **Tribal Crafting**: Leatherworking and traditional smithing align with orc culture
- **Other Classes**: Fully accessible; some orcs break tradition and become mages or traders

**Advantage**: Orc strength and tribal traditions pair exceptionally well with shamanism and martial roles, but you're not restricted to these paths. Many orc explorers find success in unexpected classes.
