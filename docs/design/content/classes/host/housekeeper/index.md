# Housekeeper

## Lore

### Origin

The Housekeeper transforms spaces from used to welcoming - the crisp sheets, the spotless floors, the subtle scent of lavender. Where innkeepers can prepare basic rooms adequately, housekeepers elevate cleanliness to an art. They know which linens last longest, how to remove any stain, and can prepare a room in half the time while achieving twice the quality.

Housekeeping is invisible excellence. When done properly, guests don't notice the work - they simply feel comfortable. They notice the perfectly made bed that looks inviting after a long day. The spotless washbasin. The fresh towels folded just so. The absence of dust, odors, or disorder. This invisibility is the mark of mastery: everything perfect, nothing intrusive.

The Housekeepers' Guild teaches efficiency through rigorous training. Perfect bed-making (hospital corners, wrinkle-free sheets). Stain removal (wine, blood, ink, mud). Organization systems (supply carts, cleaning routes, timing). These techniques enable housekeepers to maintain dozens of rooms to exacting standards.

### In the World

Every inn, manor, and boarding house needs housekeepers. The quality of lodging depends more on housekeeping than fancy furnishings. Travelers forgive plain furniture if rooms are spotless. They remember comfort long after forgetting d√©cor.

Regular guests notice the housekeeper's touch - the way towels are folded, the fresh flowers by the bedside, the perfect firmness of pillows. A skilled housekeeper doesn't just clean; they create sanctuaries. They anticipate needs: extra blankets on cold nights, lighter linens in summer, blackout curtains for those who sleep late.

Housekeepers develop systems. The morning route: strip beds, collect laundry, restock supplies. The midday clean: floors, surfaces, windows. The evening preparation: turn down beds, light candles, ensure everything ready for arrivals. Efficiency comes from repetition and optimization - every movement purposeful, every tool within reach.

Master housekeepers run entire estates. They manage junior housekeepers, coordinate with laundry services, maintain supply inventories, and ensure every room meets standards. Their reputation determines inn prestige: "The Rusty Tankard's rooms are always spotless. You could eat off the floors."

#### Synergy Skills

Skills with strong synergies for Housekeeper:

**Cleaning Skills**:

- Speed Cleaning (tiered) - Faster cleaning actions without quality loss
- Quality Eye (mechanic) - Notice and fix problems proactively
- Organization (passive) - Maintain supply inventory efficiently
- Stain Removal (tiered) - Remove progressively difficult stains

- Durability Care (common) - Extend lifespan of furnishings and linens
- Minor Repairs (common) - Fix small issues before they become problems
- Supply Management (lesser) - Track and restock cleaning materials

#### Synergy Bonuses

Housekeeper provides context-specific bonuses to lodging service skills:

**Housekeeping Skills** (Strong synergy):

- **Efficient Cleaning**: Core specialization skill

- **Stain Mastery**: Direct application
  - Faster learning: 2x XP from stain removal

- **Comfort Arrangement**: Essential skill

### Tracked Actions for XP

Housekeeper gains XP from actions tagged with `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` (and its children):

| Action Category      | Specific Actions                                  | Example Tag                   | XP Value            |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Room Preparation     | Clean, arrange, stock guest rooms                 | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 10-25 per room      |
| Linen Management     | Wash, fold, replace bedding and towels            | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 5-15 per set        |
| Facility Maintenance | Spot repairs, report issues, maintain cleanliness | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 8-20 per task       |
| Deep Cleaning        | Thorough seasonal or special cleaning             | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 30-60 per room      |
| Stain Removal        | Remove difficult stains from linens/surfaces      | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 10-30 per stain     |
| Supply Management    | Track inventory, restock supplies                 | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 5-12 per session    |

| Training             | Train junior housekeepers, demonstrate techniques | `Service/Hospitality/Lodging` | 20-50 per session   |

**Note**: Housekeeper only tracks lodging service actions - does NOT gain XP from food service or general management (unlike Innkeeper).

## MVP Integration (001-minimal-possession-demo)

**Role**: Hireable NPC specialist

**Hiring Details**:

- **Contract Cost**: 50 gold (one-time)
- **Daily Wage**: 5 gold per day
- **Efficiency**: +100% room preparation speed vs Innkeeper
- **Quality**: +30% guest comfort (higher lodging revenue)

**Core Actions** (when hired):

1. **Prepare Room** (Specialist)
   - Tag: `Service/Hospitality/Lodging`
   - Duration: 7.5 seconds (vs Innkeeper's 15 seconds)
   - Resources: -1 Linen Supply
   - Effect: Room ready for guest (generates +12 gold on check-in)
   - XP: 18 per room (Housekeeper gains more XP than Innkeeper)
   - Quality: +30% guest satisfaction (potential for premium pricing)

2. **Deep Clean Room**
   - Tag: `Service/Hospitality/Lodging`
   - Duration: 20 seconds
   - Resources: -2 Cleaning Supplies
   - Effect: Room quality upgraded to "Spotless" (+50% satisfaction, +5 gold premium)
   - XP: 35 per deep clean
   - Cooldown: Once per room per day

3. **Restock Linens**
   - Tag: `Service/Hospitality/Lodging`
   - Duration: 10 seconds
   - Resources: Transfer clean linens to room storage
   - Effect: Replenish linen supply for room preparation
   - XP: 8 per restock
   - Triggered: When linen supply < 3

4. **Remove Stain**
   - Tag: `Service/Hospitality/Lodging`
   - Duration: 15 seconds
   - Resources: -1 Cleaning Supply
   - Success Rate: 80% base (+5% per Housekeeper level)
   - Effect: Salvage stained linen (avoid replacement cost)
   - XP: 15 per successful stain removal
   - Savings: 5 gold (linen replacement cost avoided)

**Autonomous Behavior** (when not possessed):

- Prioritizes preparing empty rooms for new guests
- Performs deep cleaning during low-occupancy periods
- Automatically restocks linens when supply low
- Reports damaged furnishings to Innkeeper

**Design Notes**:

- Housekeeper is hireable specialist (not player-controlled at MVP start)
- Demonstrates specialist efficiency (2x speed vs generalist)
- Shows quality bonuses (better comfort = higher revenue)
- Autonomous AI uses same action system as possessed control
- Stain removal shows skill checks (success rate improves with level)
