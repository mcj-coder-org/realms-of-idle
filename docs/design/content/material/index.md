# Materials

Materials are the foundation of crafting and trade. Gathered by specialists, processed by crafters, and traded throughout the world.

## Categories

- **[Metals](metal/)** - Ore and refined metals for smithing
- **[Organic](organic/)** - Natural materials from flora and fauna
- **[Fuel](fuel/)** - Energy sources for processing and crafting

## Related Classes

- **[Miner](../../classes/gatherer/miner/)** - Gathers ore, stone, and minerals
- **[Lumberjack](../../classes/gatherer/lumberjack/)** - Gathers wood and organic materials
- **[Hunter](../../classes/gatherer/hunter/)** - Gathers leather and creature materials
- **[Blacksmith](../../classes/crafter/blacksmith/)** - Processes and crafts with materials
